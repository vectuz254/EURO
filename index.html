Who We Are

A Community Standing
for Each Other

Joint Hands Charity was founded on a simple belief: that every person in our community deserves dignity, opportunity, and care. Based in Kenya, we work directly alongside families in need — bringing education, food security, healthcare access, and hope to those who need it most.

We are not outsiders delivering aid. We are neighbours building futures.

What We Do

Programs That Change Lives

🎓

Education Support

Scholarships, school supplies, and mentorship programs ensuring children stay in school and thrive.

🍽️

Food Security

Regular food distributions and community kitchen programs fighting hunger in vulnerable households.

🏥

Healthcare Access

Free medical camps, health education, and partnerships with clinics to serve those without coverage.

💧

Clean Water

Borehole drilling and water purification projects bringing safe drinking water to remote villages.

👩‍👧

Women Empowerment

Skills training, microfinance groups, and entrepreneurship workshops helping women become independent.

🌱

Youth Development

Sports, arts, and vocational programmes that keep youth engaged, skilled, and hopeful for the future.
